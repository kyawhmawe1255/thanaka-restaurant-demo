Burmese Harmony Plate

Available for 1 or 2 people

Inspired by the vibrant sharing feasts enjoyed in Burmese households, each Harmony Plate features a curated selection of our family's authentic recipes, offering a delightful journey through Myanmar's signature sweet, sour, salty, spicy, and bitter flavors.

What is included

A selection of curries, traditional salads, steamed rice, soup of the day, and a refreshing homemade lemonade. Every plate is thoughtfully prepared to offer a balanced and authentic Burmese dining experience.
